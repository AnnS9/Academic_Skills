Women Empowerment Principles

There are many organization who Support the principles of women's empowerment. These principles are the result of a collaboration between the UN Global Compact and UN Women and have been adapted from Calvert Women's Principles. These organizations collect data from real business practices from around the world.

The principles case for companies' efforts is to promote gender equality and women's empowerment. Referring to the United Nations Global Compact We can get more familiar, with these principles to empower women in the workplace, in the marketplace, and in the community.
According to the UN Global Compact There are 7 main Principles that in courage of woman position in the business fundamental structures like also in society.
    Principle 1:
    Establish high-level corporate gender equality leadership.
    Principle 2:
    Fair treatment of all women and men at work - respect and support human rights and non-discrimination.
    Principle 3:
    Ensure the health, safety and well-being of all female and male workers.
    Principle 4:
    Promote female education, training and professional development.
    Principle 5:
    Implement enterprise development, supply chain, and marketing practices that empower women.
    Principle 6:
    Promote equality through community initiatives and advocacy.
    Principle 7:
    Measure progress in gender equality and publicize it.
 To find out more about Women empowerment visit: United Nations Global Compact
