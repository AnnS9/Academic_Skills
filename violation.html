What is women Empowerment?

Referring to the ‘Amnesty International’ Women’s rights are human rights, which are all entitled to the same rights of humans. These include the right to live free from violence and discrimination and enjoy the highest attainable standard of physical and mental health, like also to be educated, own a property have rights to vote and to earn an equal wage.
